FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

We've stripped away the bureaucracy. Here's what most partners ask us before getting started.

How does Golden Globe accelerate underwriting?+
Direct partner review. Senior-level credit committees. No algorithmic bottlenecks. Every file is touched by a decision-maker from day one.
What asset classes do you accept as collateral?+
We accept Tier 1 real estate, public and private equity, alternative assets including collectibles, fine art, and structured portfolios. Reach out for a bespoke assessment.
What is the minimum capital requirement?+
Our mandates typically begin at $2M. We do not operate in the retail lending space — Golden Globe is exclusively for high-net-worth individuals and institutional partners.
Is my financial information kept confidential?+
Absolutely. All data flows through our Obsidian-grade encrypted portal. We operate under strict NDA and institutional-grade privacy protocols. Your information never leaves our secure infrastructure.
